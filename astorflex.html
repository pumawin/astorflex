Material

  • Natural Rubber
    Most of our soles are in natural rubber consisting in coagulated latex from the rubber tree (hevea brasilianis).
  • Water-Based Adhesives
    The various shoe parts are assembled using water-based adhesives
  • Vegetable-Tanned Calf Leather
    The lining and insoles are in vegetable-tanned calf leather, obtained by soaking the leather in a mixture of water and powdered oak bark and mimosa, without using dies or color finishes. This process makes the leather healthier, hipoallergenic and guarantees greater sweat absortion for dry, odourless feet.

History

My name is Fabio Travenzoli and i own Astorflex a company who’s name was invented by my father Alfredo who, together with his brother Bettino, succeeded in the business of their father Daniele and of their grandfather Ferdinando.After the first world war, at the outset of industrialisation, the small “Fratelli Travenzoli” concern started to produce different types of footwear using the “Ideal” method (outer stitching along the edge of the sole).That i have pursued since 1984
