Revitalize your Astorflex shoes

with these simple tips
for cleaning leather uppers.

  • Soft-bristled brush

  • Clean, dry cloth

  • Leather cleaner
    (preferably a pH-balanced one)

  • Leather conditioner

  • Water and a bowl
    (for cleaning suede uppers)

  • Suede eraser and brush
    (for cleaning suede uppers)

Step-by-step guide to

cleaning leather uppers

    Suede uppers require a different cleaning approach than leather uppers. Here’s how to clean suede uppers:

  • Use a suede eraser to remove any stains or marks on the suede uppers. Rub the eraser gently in a circular motion until the stain is removed.

  • Use a suede brush to restore the nap of the suede. Brush the suede in one direction to avoid damaging the fibers.

  • If the stain is still visible, you can clean the suede uppers using a mixture of water and white vinegar. Mix equal parts water and vinegar in a bowl and apply the mixture to the stain using a soft-bristled brush. Blot the suede with a clean cloth and let it air dry.

    Step-by-step guide to cleaning leather uppers

  • Brush off any dirt or dust from the leather uppers using a soft-bristled brush.

  • Dampen a clean cloth with water and wring it out. Apply a small amount of leather cleaner to the cloth and gently rub it onto the leather uppers. Avoid using too much cleaner as it may damage the leather.

  • Wipe off the cleaner with a clean, damp cloth.

  • Let the shoes air dry.

  • Apply a small amount of leather conditioner to a clean, dry cloth and rub it onto the leather uppers in circular motions. This will help to restore the leather’s natural oils and prevent it from drying out and cracking.

  • Let the shoes sit for a few minutes to allow the conditioner to absorb into the leather.

  • Wipe off any excess conditioner with a clean, dry cloth.
